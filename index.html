Literature

Greenhouses are constructed to protect plants from severe weather and to offer year-round temperature. Growing plants or vegetables in a building with walls and a roof made mostly of transparent material is known as greenhouse farming. Considering about the previous research projects done related to the greenhouse farming. Eco-Harvest is a smart greenhouse monitoring system for greenhouse capsicum farming with new features that are not available in previous research projects.

The website discusses the implementation of urban farming through IoT technology with a focus on monitoring and optimizing environmental conditions in greenhouses. It highlights the use of IoT sensors to monitor soil moisture, humidity, temperature and light intensity. The site also introduces a real-time system that uses image processing to measure plant growth and to measure harvest quality. To address the challenges of disease detection, the site includes image processing techniques and plant disease detection. It provides users with informative reports on plant diseases and provides guidance on disease control and treatment. This comprehensive platform aims to improve urban farming efficiency and crop yields.

Solution

The solution proposed is a smart greenhouse monitoring and controling system which has the capabilities of monitoring and controling temperature, soil moisture, and light intensity which can be controlled automatically or manually form a mobile application , monitoring the Plant growth, plant diseases and harvest quality using image processing through a web application.

No Automated systems to measure the harvest.

Farmers have to work hard to measure the of the harvest and the information gathered may not be 100% accurate.

Incapable of regulating microclimate parameters and monitoring the greenhouse.

Temperature, soil humidity, and light intensity in a greenhouse are challenging to maintain in balance and to monitor and manage.

Unavailability of a system to detect capsicum plant diseases.

Diseases need to be identified for the protection of the harvest and should be treated as soon as they are identified.

Identifying the quality of the capsicum plant.​

Measuring the quality of plant will be important to identify whether the crops are properly grown to sell in the market.

Main Objective

  • Develop a mobile application for identifying and controling microclimate factors and a web application to measure plant growth, plant quality and to identify plant diseases.

    • Sub-Objectives

    • Harvest measurement with image processing. ​
    • Measure and manage temperature, light intensity, and soil moisture properties with IoT .​
    • Plant growth monitoring with image processing ​
    • Plant disease detection with image processing .
